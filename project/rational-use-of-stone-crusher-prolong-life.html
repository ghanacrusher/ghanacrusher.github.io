Rational use of stone crusher extend the use of time

Substantially all of the mechanical equipment is not able to accept the long-term overload operation, although the grass may be in line with the operating business temporary benefits, but in the long-term perspective, is not a wise move. CAG appeal to everyone, do not allow the operation of a stone crusher equipment overload, overload due to long-term operation of a stone crusher will greatly reduce its life, but also the degree of wear of parts will increase, and therefore should be adjusted stones workload crusher, when aging equipment also should be replaced; if the stone crusher host Super negative long-term operation, aging equipment will cause serious consequences such as bending.

CAG remind the majority of users in the use of stone crusher equipment during the compressive strength of the material should be in accordance with the choice of an appropriate work intensity, to avoid overload stone crusher equipment, and should always check the damage equipment wearing parts, It should be found to replace the damage in time to avoid the impact of stone crusher equipment operating efficiency and product quality, thereby affecting the overall functioning and efficiency of the production line profits.
