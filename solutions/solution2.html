Infrastructure to bring the dawn of Chinese crusher industry

China's automation technology started relatively late, most of the development process in order to rely on foreign advanced technology, many customers used to the big brands, domestic brands do not want to try crusher products. Similar vicious circle like shackles restricting the development of Chinese technology, making the capability of independent innovation is weak crusher industrial technology.

Currently, high-yield, high efficiency, high-tech crusher in our country only a few manufacturers, compared with developed countries phase, the overall competitiveness and development potential are still unable to compete with the developed countries, the current domestic high-end users and export products supporting the basic components mainly rely on imports.

Our crusher manufacturing industry market is very broad, including various chemical industry, mining, construction, water conservancy, metallurgy, coal, glass and the like. In China the most important field of application is the cement industry, paving and mining, applications in these two sectors accounted for the crusher each about 30% of the entire industry. In recent years, according to the development of China's industry, mining machinery crusher industry has become an important industry in the process of industrialization. With urban construction in China entered a period of development, as a high-speed rail development in basic materials - sand and gravel aggregate production industry also will be developed, crusher equipment companies unprecedented opportunities.

In the town of competition in one fell swoop, to shorten the gap with the developed countries of the time.

Crusher innovation

Technological Innovation crusher industry is a difficult and long-term technical activities, we must be closely integrated mining enterprise development, grasp the development situation is good demand and technology.
